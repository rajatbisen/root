Introduction: Vivanta is an Indian hotel chain established in September 2010. The brand is a part of the Indian Hotels Company Limited, a subsidiary of the TATA Group. The brand Vivanta was born as a part of The Indian Hotels Company Limited brand architecture exercise. With this the brand rolled over 19 of its hotels to the new brand. This brand architecture exercise was a part of their previous launch of The Gateway hotels, which is marketed as an upscale brand.

Some Flabbergastic Locations:
Taj Lake palace,Udaipur:
This romantic white-marble palace floating on the turquoise waters of Lake Pichola, voted as one of the best luxury hotels, is opulence at its best. The property features open-air courtyards, lotus ponds and a small mango tree shaded pool to complete its majestic architecture. Special Features: For an ultimate rejuvenating experience, treat your love and yourself with a Jiva Spa. This boat with a relaxation lounge takes you amidst the lake and pampers you with massages scrubs and wraps. Tariff: INR 25,000 – INR 5,75,000!
Taj Falaknuma Palace,Hyderabad:
Special Features: This lavish palace hotel has 60 uniquely distinct rooms furnished with floral fabrics, fine wood fittings and Egyptian cotton linen. The magnificent oak-panelled, palace library, is a replica of the library at Windsor Castle and encloses rare manuscripts. The palace boasts of the rich heritage walks with beautifully crafted objects from the world over; Mughal, Rajasthani and Japanese gardens personally conceived by the Nizam and the grand dining rooms. Tariff: INR 33,000 – INR 1,75,000!
Umaid Bhawan Palace,Jodhpur:
Special Features: The contemporary palace hotel is an extravagant property of the Taj group. The hotel interiors are embellished in art deco style, equipped with modern conveniences. A special feature is their Champagne Museum Walk, where the guests can admire the finest collection of Victorian and Edwardian antiques with a glass of the most expensive champagnes. The hotel offers specialised dining experiences & romantic settings with spectacular views from balconies & terraces. Tip: Do not miss a ride in the vintage horse driven carriage at the Umaid Bhawan Palace. Tariff: INR 39,000 – INR 4,00,000!
